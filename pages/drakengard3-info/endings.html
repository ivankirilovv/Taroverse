Ending A: Accord

Ending A, also known as the Accord Ending, serves as the true conclusion to Drakengard 3, offering players a culmination of the game's complex narrative threads and character arcs. As protagonist Zero confronts her destiny and the true nature of the Intoners, players are thrust into a climactic battle against her sisters and the forces of darkness. Depending on the player's actions throughout the game, Ending A offers different outcomes, each with its own implications for the fate of the world and its inhabitants. The Accord Ending delves deep into the themes of sacrifice, redemption, and the cyclical nature of violence, challenging players to consider the consequences of their choices and the true cost of power.

Ending B: Zero

Ending B, also known as the Zero Ending, delves into the consequences of Zero's actions and the toll they have taken on her and her companions. As Zero confronts her sisters and the true source of the world's chaos, players are presented with a choice that will determine the fate of the world and Zero herself. Ending B explores themes of identity, mortality, and the pursuit of power, offering a dark and introspective conclusion to Zero's journey. Through its haunting imagery and thought-provoking narrative, Ending B leaves players with a sense of ambiguity and introspection as they contemplate the nature of fate and the choices that define us.

Ending C: One's Prologue

Ending C, also known as the One's Prologue Ending, offers players a glimpse into the origins of the Intoners and the events leading up to the first game in the Drakengard series. Set against the backdrop of a world on the brink of destruction, Ending C provides valuable insight into the history of the world and the characters introduced in Drakengard 3. Through its evocative storytelling and rich lore, Ending C sets the stage for the dark and epic tale that follows, inviting players to explore the mysteries of the past and uncover the truths that lie hidden beneath the surface.
