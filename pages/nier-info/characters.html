Playable Characters

NieR

NieR, the central protagonist, is a steadfast and devoted father (or brother, depending on the version played) determined to save his daughter/sister, Yonah, from the grips of a mysterious and incurable illness known as the Black Scrawl. His character is defined by his unwavering love for his family and his willingness to brave any danger to ensure their safety. Despite the hardships he faces in a world overrun by monstrous creatures called Shades, NieR remains resolute and compassionate, embodying the resilience of the human spirit.

Kainé

Kainé is a fiercely independent and resilient warrior cursed with a half-human, half-Shade hybrid form. Her appearance and brash demeanor often shock those around her, leading to her isolation from society. Beneath her rough exterior, however, Kainé harbors a deeply compassionate heart and a strong sense of justice. Despite her own struggles and inner demons, she forms a powerful bond with NieR, joining him on his journey to confront the darkness threatening their world. Kainé's complex backstory and inner turmoil add layers of depth to her character, making her a compelling and multifaceted protagonist.

Emil

Emil is a kind-hearted and innocent young boy cursed with the tragic ability to turn anything he gazes upon to stone. Despite the loneliness and isolation brought about by his condition, Emil maintains an unwavering optimism and a strong desire to help others. His purity of heart and gentle demeanor make him a beloved companion to NieR and Kainé as they navigate the dangers of their world together. Despite the darkness that surrounds him, Emil serves as a source of light and hope amidst the shadows, embodying the resilience of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Unplayable Characters

Yonah

Yonah, NieR's beloved daughter (or sister), is afflicted by the devastating Black Scrawl disease, which threatens to claim her life. Despite her fragile health, Yonah possesses a gentle and optimistic nature, serving as a beacon of hope for NieR as he embarks on his quest to find a cure. Her unwavering belief in her father's ability to save her fuels NieR's determination, driving him forward in the face of seemingly insurmountable odds.

Devola and Popola

Devola and Popola are twin androids who serve as caretakers and guides for NieR throughout his journey. They provide valuable assistance and information to NieR, often acting as liaisons between him and the remnants of human civilization. Despite their serene and composed demeanor, Devola and Popola harbor their own secrets and regrets, adding layers of complexity to their characters.

Grimoire Weiss

Grimoire Weiss is a sentient, talking book imbued with powerful magical abilities. Initially portrayed as arrogant and aloof, Weiss gradually reveals layers of complexity and vulnerability beneath his stoic exterior as NieR's journey unfolds. Despite his initial reluctance to aid NieR, Weiss ultimately becomes a loyal ally, providing invaluable magical support and knowledge as they confront the dangers of their world together. As NieR and Weiss' relationship deepens, Weiss undergoes his own journey of self-discovery, ultimately proving to be a crucial ally in their quest to save Yonah and unravel the mysteries of the world around them.

Shadowlord

The Shadowlord is the primary antagonist of NieR, serving as a dark mirror to NieR himself. He is revealed to be a twisted version of NieR from an alternate timeline, driven by his own desperate desire to save his own daughter, causing him to clash with NieR and his companions in a tragic and emotionally charged confrontation.
