Ending A: The Lies of the World

In Ending A, players experience the initial conclusion to NieR's main storyline. As they delve deeper into the game's narrative, they uncover the truth behind protagonist Nier's quest to save his daughter, Yonah, from the deadly Black Scrawl disease. The journey is fraught with sacrifices, revelations, and moral dilemmas, culminating in a bittersweet resolution that challenges players to consider the true cost of their actions. Ending A explores themes of love, loss, and the lengths a parent will go to protect their child, leaving players with a sense of closure tempered by the weight of their choices.

Ending B: Kainé

Ending B offers players an opportunity to delve deeper into the backstory of Kainé, one of Nier's key companions on his journey. As players complete additional quests and unlock new story elements, they uncover Kainé's tragic past and the events that shaped her into the person she is in the game's present. Ending B adds depth and nuance to Kainé's character, highlighting her resilience, vulnerability, and unwavering determination in the face of adversity. Through Kainé's story, players gain insight into the complexities of the game's world and the struggles of its inhabitants.

Ending C: The Lost World

Ending C serves as the true conclusion to NieR's main storyline, offering players a culmination of the game's narrative threads and character arcs. As Nier and his companions confront the final challenges standing between them and their goals, players are presented with a choice that will determine the fate of the world and its inhabitants. Ending C explores themes of fate, redemption, and the power of human connection, challenging players to confront the consequences of their actions and the true cost of salvation. The ending leaves players with a sense of closure and reflection as they contemplate the impact of their choices on the world and its inhabitants.

Ending D: Mermaid

Ending D provides players with a glimpse into the lives of the game's supporting characters and the impact of Nier's actions on their world. As players explore the game's world and interact with its inhabitants, they uncover new storylines and hidden secrets that shed light on the events of the main storyline. Ending D offers closure to the game's side quests and character arcs, providing a sense of completion to players who choose to delve deeper into the game's lore and narrative.

Ending E: The Little Mermaid

Ending E serves as a bonus conclusion to NieR, offering players a lighthearted and whimsical epilogue to the game's main storyline. As players complete specific tasks and fulfill certain conditions, they unlock new story elements and interactions with the game's characters. Ending E provides a sense of closure and satisfaction to players who have invested time and effort into completing the game's various challenges and side quests, rewarding them with a memorable and heartwarming conclusion.
