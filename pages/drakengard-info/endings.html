Ending A: The Seals are Broken

In Ending A, protagonist Caim and his dragon partner, Angelus, successfully defeat the Empire's forces and break the seals holding the world together. However, in their pursuit of victory, Caim becomes consumed by vengeance and loses his humanity, ultimately leading to a tragic conclusion. The ending sets the stage for the events of the game's sequels and spin-offs, establishing a dark and foreboding tone for the series.

Ending B: Inuart's Ambition

Ending B explores the consequences of Inuart's betrayal and his desire to save his beloved Furiae. Despite Caim's efforts to stop him, Inuart's actions ultimately lead to his own downfall and the unleashing of a catastrophic force that threatens to consume the world. The ending highlights the themes of betrayal, ambition, and the tragic consequences of unchecked power.

Ending C: The Seeds of Resurrection

Ending C reveals the true nature of the Grotesqueries and the dark forces lurking beneath the surface of the world. As Caim and his companions delve deeper into the mysteries surrounding the Grotesqueries, they uncover a sinister plot to resurrect an ancient and malevolent deity. The ending culminates in a climactic battle against the forces of darkness, with the fate of the world hanging in the balance.

Ending D: Growing Wings

Ending D offers a glimpse into the aftermath of Caim's quest for vengeance and the toll it has taken on his soul. As Caim confronts the consequences of his actions, he is faced with a choice that will determine the fate of the world and all who inhabit it. The ending explores themes of redemption, sacrifice, and the power of forgiveness, leaving players with a sense of hope amidst the darkness.

Ending E: The End of the World

Ending E serves as the true conclusion to "Drakengard," revealing the full extent of the devastation wrought by Caim's actions and the dark forces he unleashed. As the world teeters on the brink of destruction, Caim and his companions must confront their inner demons and make the ultimate sacrifice to save what remains of humanity. The ending is a haunting and poignant finale to the game's dark and epic tale, leaving players with a sense of awe and contemplation.
