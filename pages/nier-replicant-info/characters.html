Playable Characters

NieR (Brother)

The Brother, also known as NieR, is the main protagonist controlled by the player throughout NieR Replicant ver.1.22474487139... His journey revolves around finding a cure for his sister, Yonah, who suffers from the deadly Black Scrawl disease. As players guide the Brother through the game's events, they witness his unwavering determination, his deep love for his sister, and the emotional toll of the trials he faces in the process. The Brother is characterized by his selflessness, bravery, and willingness to sacrifice everything to save the ones he loves.

Unplayable Characters

Yonah

Yonah is the younger sister of the protagonist, afflicted by the fatal Black Scrawl disease. She serves as the primary motivation driving the Brother's quest, as he desperately seeks a cure to save her life. Yonah's gentle demeanor, unwavering optimism, and strong bond with her brother add emotional depth to the narrative of NieR Replicant ver.1.22474487139..., highlighting the themes of family, sacrifice, and the resilience of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Grimoire Weiss

Grimoire Weiss is a sentient, talking book endowed with powerful magical abilities, serving as a companion and ally to the protagonist on his journey. Despite his initially aloof and condescending demeanor, Weiss proves to be a valuable asset to the Brother, providing him with magical support and knowledge throughout their quest. As players interact with Weiss, they uncover the mysteries surrounding his origins and his connection to the events unfolding around them, adding layers of intrigue and complexity to his character.

Kainé

Kainé is a fierce and resilient warrior cursed with a half-human, half-Shade hybrid form, joining the Brother on his journey in NieR Replicant ver.1.22474487139... Her rough exterior and foul-mouthed demeanor belie a deeply compassionate heart and a strong sense of justice. Despite her own struggles and inner demons, Kainé forms a powerful bond with the Brother as they confront the dangers of their world together. As players delve deeper into Kainé's backstory, they uncover the trauma and pain that have shaped her character, adding layers of depth and emotional resonance to her portrayal.

Emil

Emil is a kind-hearted and innocent young boy cursed with the ability to turn anything he gazes upon to stone, accompanying the Brother and Kainé on their journey in NieR Replicant ver.1.22474487139... His purity of heart, gentle demeanor, and unwavering optimism make him a beloved companion to the Brother, serving as a source of light and hope amidst the darkness of their world. As players interact with Emil, they witness his growth and development as he grapples with the consequences of his unique abilities and his place in the world.
