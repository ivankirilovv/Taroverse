Ending A: The Glory of Mankind

Ending A serves as the primary conclusion to NieR: Automata's main storyline. As players guide android protagonists 2B, 9S, and A2 through the game's events, they uncover the truth behind the war between androids and machines and the mysteries of humanity's fate. Ending A provides closure to the game's central narrative, exploring themes of identity, purpose, and the nature of consciousness. It leaves players with a sense of satisfaction and reflection as they witness the culmination of the characters' journeys and the resolution of the conflicts they faced.

Ending B: or not to [B]

Ending B offers players a unique perspective on the events of NieR: Automata, allowing them to experience the game's narrative from the viewpoint of another key character, 9S. As players delve deeper into 9S's story and motivations, they gain new insights into the world of the game and the relationships between its characters. Ending B provides a deeper understanding of the game's themes and conflicts, challenging players to reconsider their assumptions and interpretations of the events they have witnessed.

Ending C: meaningless code

Ending C presents players with a dramatic and emotional conclusion to NieR: Automata, offering a heartbreaking resolution to the game's central conflicts. As players navigate the game's final moments, they are faced with a choice that will determine the fate of the game's protagonists and the world they inhabit. Ending C explores themes of sacrifice, redemption, and the inherent contradictions of existence. It leaves players with a profound sense of loss and contemplation as they grapple with the consequences of their decisions.

Ending D: [E]nd of YoRHa

Ending D serves as an epilogue to NieR: Automata, offering players a glimpse into the aftermath of the game's main storyline. As players explore the game's world and interact with its characters, they uncover new storylines and hidden secrets that shed light on the events of the main narrative. Ending D provides closure to the game's side quests and character arcs, offering players a sense of completion and satisfaction as they bid farewell to the world of NieR: Automata.

Ending E: the [E]nd of YoRHa

Ending E serves as a bonus conclusion to NieR: Automata, rewarding players who have invested time and effort into completing the game's various challenges and side quests. As players embark on one final journey through the game's world, they are treated to a heartwarming and uplifting epilogue that celebrates the bonds of friendship and the resilience of the human spirit. Ending E provides a sense of closure and catharsis to players who have accompanied the game's characters on their journey. It leaves them with a lasting sense of fulfillment and joy, reinforcing the emotional impact of the game's themes and narrative.
