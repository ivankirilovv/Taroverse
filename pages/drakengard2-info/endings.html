Ending A: The Future of the Seal

In Ending A, protagonist Nowe confronts the true nature of his existence and the consequences of his actions. Throughout his journey, Nowe battles against the forces of darkness alongside his companions, seeking to restore balance to the world. As the story reaches its climax, Nowe faces his greatest challenge yet, coming face-to-face with the source of the world's corruption. With the help of his allies, Nowe overcomes this final obstacle and ultimately seals away the darkness, bringing peace to the land. Ending A highlights themes of redemption, sacrifice, and the power of friendship, offering a sense of closure to Nowe's journey as he embraces his role as a protector of the world.

Ending B: The Fates of the Divine

Ending B delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the Divine Seeds and the dark forces threatening the world. As Nowe and his allies uncover the truth behind the Divine Seeds, they are faced with a choice that will determine the fate of the world and all who inhabit it. The ending explores themes of destiny, free will, and the consequences of one's choices as Nowe grapples with the weight of his decisions. With multiple endings depending on player choices throughout the game, Ending B offers a sense of uncertainty and reflection as players consider the implications of their actions on the world and its inhabitants.

Ending C: Wings of the Valiant

Ending C serves as the true conclusion to Drakengard 2, revealing the full extent of the sacrifices made by Nowe and his companions in their quest to save the world. As the forces of darkness close in, Nowe must confront his own inner demons and make the ultimate sacrifice to ensure the survival of humanity. The ending is a bittersweet finale to the game's epic tale, leaving players with a sense of both loss and hope for the future. Ending C encapsulates the themes of heroism, selflessness, and the enduring strength of the human spirit, offering a poignant and memorable conclusion to Nowe's journey.
